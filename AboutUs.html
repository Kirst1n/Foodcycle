Who are we?

We are a group of passionate, environmentally-cautious, driven ladies with a desire to make the world a better place. The idea is simple: minimise food waste & maximise good taste. Based in St Andrews, we aim to provide all our customers and our providers with an easy platform off of which restaurants and supermarkets can advertise their food still remaining on their shelves for reduced prices - food that would otherwise end up wasted.
